💧 Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration helps maintain body temperature, lubricate joints, and transport nutrients.
😴 Get Enough Sleep
Adults need 7-9 hours of sleep per night. Good sleep improves memory, mood, and overall health.
🥗 Eat Balanced Diet
Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your daily meals for optimal health.
🏃 Exercise Regularly
At least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily can reduce risk of heart disease, diabetes, and improve mental health.
